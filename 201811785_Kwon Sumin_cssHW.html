Sitcoms

Friends

'Friends' is a legendary sitcom that describes the life and friendship of three women and three men. It contains 10 seasons and each of the episodes is about 20 minutes long. Once you start watching it, you won't be able to stop!

  • 10 Seasons
  • 1994.9.22 ~ 2004.5.6
Brooklyn 99

'Brooklyn 99' shows all the things that take place in the imaginary ninety-ninth precinct in New York. Every line is golden and the characters are hilarious. Currently, the eighth season is being shot, which is officially announced as the last season.

  • 7 Seasons (Season 8 being shooted)
  • 2013.9.17 ~

Dramas

The Good Place

'The Good Place' is about after-life. The main character Eleanor Shellstrop is misplaced in the 'good place' instead of the 'bad place', and you get to join the whole journey she and her friends go through for 4 seasons. Laughter, tears, and lessons are guaranteed.

  • 4 Seasons
  • 2016.9.19 ~ 2020.1.30
The School Nurse Files

A creepy yet funny Netflix Original Series based on a novel by Chung Serang. It is about a school nurse who can see jelly ghosts. Even though it is based on an unrealistic world view, the characters are portrayed very realistically.

  • 1 Season
  • 2020.9.25
